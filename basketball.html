Defense

Enhance your defensive skills to prevent opponents from scoring.

Drives

Penetrating the defense by driving towards the basket while dribbling. This often involves using quick changes of direction and speed to evade defenders.

Layups

A close-range shot made while moving toward the basket, often performed with one hand. Layups are essential for scoring in the paint.

Rebounding

The ability to gain possession of the ball after a missed shot. Rebounding can be either offensive (recovering the ball on the attack) or defensive (recovering the ball on defense).

Behind-The-Back-Dribble

Dribbling the ball behind the back to protect it from a defender and change direction.

Spin-Move

A maneuver where the player spins around a defender while dribbling, often used to create space for a shot or drive.
