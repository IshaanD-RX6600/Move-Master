Hi there! We’re two grade 10 students who have recently discovered our passion for coding. In just six months, we’ve dived into the world of CSS, HTML, and JavaScript, and we’re excited to share our very first project with you: MoveMaster.

What is MoveMaster?

MoveMaster is your go-to app for mastering sports moves, one step at a time. Whether you’re a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced athlete refining your skills, MoveMaster guides you through drills and exercises designed to help you improve and excel. We break down complex moves into simple, manageable steps, making it easy for anyone to progress from novice to expert.
